About BigHat Fabrication

It all started in 2018 when my wife asked me if I could make her a kitchen table. That simple request turned into barn doors, fireplaces, mantles, cabinets, bookshelves, entertainment centers, fancy dog kennels, planter boxes, desks, conference tables, pantries, closets, laundry rooms, mudrooms, garage storage, workshops, storage rooms, TV mounts, projectors, Sonos systems, security cameras, smart lighting/shades, home networking, and a 60ft long retaining wall with built in planter boxes and an outdoor sectional I lovingly call "The Ark". If you're looking for a custom solution for your home or office reach out and let us obsess over the details. Thanks! -Jimmy
